History Of Pakistan

Pre-Independence Era

"Before the birth of Pakistan, Muslims in the Indian subcontinent struggled for their identity, rights, and freedom. Guided by leaders like Allama Iqbal and Quaid-e-Azam, the Two-Nation Theory became the foundation for a separate homeland."

Independence (1947)

"On 14 August 1947, Pakistan emerged as an independent nation after decades of struggle. Millions migrated to the new country, facing hardships but holding onto hope for a better future."

Early Years of Pakistan

"In its early years, Pakistan worked hard to build its institutions, economy, and unity. Despite challenges, the spirit of sacrifice and patriotism shaped the nation's foundation."
